Research Collaboration in Interactive Techniques

Join our research collaboration in Interactive Techniques, focusing on user experience advancements in Information Visualization, Mobile Interfaces, and Wearable Technology.

Application Requirements

  • Background in UX research, interaction design, or related fields.
  • Experience in conducting or supporting research studies.
  • Interest in collaboration and contributing to advanced research projects.
  • Ability to work independently and meet research milestones.

Collaboration Details

If you are interested, please contact us at ali.neshati@ontariotechu.ca with the following:

  • Brief resume highlighting relevant experience and skills.
  • Statement of Interest in one or more of the listed research areas.
  • Optional: Examples of previous research or UX projects.
